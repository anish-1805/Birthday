VIDEO FOLDER - INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Place your video message in this folder!

REQUIRED VIDEO FILE:
--------------------
Name: birthday-message.mp4
Format: MP4 (recommended) or WEBM

HOW TO PREPARE YOUR VIDEO:
---------------------------
1. Record your video message on your phone or camera
2. Keep it between 1-3 minutes (sweet and personal)
3. Make sure the video is well-lit and audio is clear
4. Transfer the video to your computer

VIDEO TIPS:
-----------
- Speak from the heart - be genuine and emotional
- Look at the camera like you're talking to her
- Smile and show your emotions
- You can include:
  * Birthday wishes
  * What she means to you
  * Favorite memories together
  * Future plans and dreams
  * Why you love her

CONVERTING VIDEO FORMAT:
-------------------------
If your video is not in MP4 format, you can convert it online:
- CloudConvert: https://cloudconvert.com/
- Online-Convert: https://www.online-convert.com/
- Or use VLC Media Player (free software)

RECOMMENDED VIDEO SETTINGS:
----------------------------
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- File size: Under 100MB for faster loading
- Orientation: Landscape (horizontal) preferred

HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
After she blows out the birthday candles on the website,
there will be confetti animation, and then your video
message will automatically appear in a beautiful overlay!

She can:
- Play/pause the video
- Adjust volume
- Watch it multiple times
- Close it and reopen by blowing candles again

ALTERNATIVE:
------------
If you don't have a video ready, you can also:
- Use a YouTube video (you'll need to modify the code)
- Skip the video feature for now
- Add it later before her birthday

PRIVACY NOTE:
-------------
If you deploy this website publicly, anyone with the link
can see the video. If you want it private:
- Use password protection on your hosting
- Share the link only with her
- Or show it to her locally on your device
