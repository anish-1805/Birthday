Quick Way to Generate Placeholder Images:

Use these free online tools to create placeholder images for testing:

Option 1: Placeholder.com

Download images from: https://placeholder.com/

  • Recommended size: 1200x800
  • Choose pink colors (#ff69b4)
  • Add text labels like "Landing Photo", "Chapter 1", etc.

Option 2: Use Your Own Photos

Simply rename your photos to match these names:

  • landing-photo.jpg
  • chapter1-photo1.jpg
  • chapter2-photo1.jpg
  • chapter3-photo1.jpg
  • chapter4-photo1.jpg
  • chapter5-photo1.jpg
  • gallery1.jpg through gallery6.jpg

Option 3: Use Unsplash (Free Stock Photos)

Download romantic/couple photos from: https://unsplash.com/

Quick Test

For immediate testing, you can use any images you have and just rename them to match the required names above.
