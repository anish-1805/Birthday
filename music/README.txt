MUSIC FOLDER - INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Add background music to make the experience even more special!

REQUIRED MUSIC FILE:
--------------------
Name: birthday-song.mp3
Format: MP3

MUSIC SUGGESTIONS:
------------------
- Your song (the song that's special to both of you)
- Her favorite song
- A romantic instrumental
- A birthday song
- Any meaningful song from your relationship

HOW TO ADD MUSIC:
-----------------
1. Choose your song
2. Convert it to MP3 format if needed
3. Rename it to: birthday-song.mp3
4. Place it in this folder

MUSIC CONTROLS:
---------------
- A music toggle button appears in the bottom-right corner
- She can turn music on/off anytime
- Music loops continuously when playing

CONVERTING TO MP3:
------------------
If your song is not in MP3 format:
- Online MP3 Converter: https://online-audio-converter.com/
- Or use iTunes/Windows Media Player to convert

TIPS:
-----
- Choose a song with good memories
- Make sure it's not too loud or too quiet
- Instrumental versions work great as background music
- The song should match the romantic/celebratory mood

FILE SIZE:
----------
- Keep it under 10MB for faster loading
- 3-5 minutes is a good length

OPTIONAL:
---------
If you don't want background music, just leave this folder empty.
The website will work perfectly fine without it!
